Megalodon

The ultimate food for the hungry pirate

When sailing the seas, one must stay in top shape.

This Hunter's Call prized meat will give you the edge to wrestle any treasure from others hands.

Ingredients

  • Megalodon meat, from any variant of Megalodon

Preparation

  • Cooking the meat
    1. Start a fire in your cooking station.
    2. Place the meat in the pan.
    3. Cook for at least 2 minutes.
    4. Once the meat gains a golden toasted color, take out of the pan.
    5. Enjoy!
