Sweet Roll

The favorite dessert of Prisoners everywhere

This delectable pastry has become a staple of the diet of a multitude of heroes accross the ages.

Whether you are Daedra, Dragonborn or Vestige, this sugary snack will find a way to your inventory!

Ingredients

Sweet Roll

  • 1/2 tsp of Salt Pile
  • 1/4 cup of Jug of Milk
  • 2 cups of Sack of Flour
  • 2 sticks (1 cup) of Butter
  • 5 Chicken's Eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp of Extract of Vanilla Plant
  • 1 2/3 cups of Moon Sugar*

* If your friendly Khajiit traveling merchant is out of town, granulated sugar is an acceptable replacement.

Glaze

  • 4 TB of Butter
  • 2 TB of Jug of Milk
  • 2 cups Powdered Moon Sugar**
  • 1 tsp Extract from Vanilla Plant

** Again, if not available, Confectioner's Sugar will suffice.

Preparation

  • Step 1: Gather the ingredients
    1. Gather your ingredients and allow them to reach room temperature. Preheat your hearth to 350 degrees F.
    2. Cream the butter and sugar togheter and beat until fluffy (at least 5 minutes).
    3. Slowly add in each chicken's egg and beat well. Add the milk or cream.
    4. Add the flour, salt and vanilla extract and stir gently until mixed.
    5. Pour into greased sweet roll pans and bake until golden, about 20 minutes. Allow to cool.
  • Step 2: Glaze and consume
    1. For the glaze, melt the butter and add in the milk.
    2. Slowly mix in the powdered sugar and vanilla. Drizzle over cooled sweet rolls.
    3. Do not fight any dragons for 30 minutes after consuming more than 5 sweet rolls.
