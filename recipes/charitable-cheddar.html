Charitable Cheddar

The gift of chesse

A great gift for the rats of Azeroth in this trying times.

Offer it to gain a temporary follower!

Recipe for 5 servings.

Ingredients

  • Fine Aged Cheddar (5 pieces)
  • Spicy Sharp Cheddar (2 pieces)
  • Zocalo Cheddar
  • Gold Bar

Preparation

  • Making the cheese
    1. Gather your cheeses.
    2. Go to your preferred crafting station.
    3. Prepare the cheese.
    4. Enjoy your Charitable Cheddar!
